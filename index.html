🚨 The Problem

Millions of gig workers depend on daily earnings for survival. However, their income is highly unstable and vulnerable to external disruptions such as heavy rainfall, traffic congestion, strikes, or technical outages in delivery platforms.

Unlike salaried employees, gig workers lack job security, insurance, and paid leave. A single day of disruption can directly impact their ability to afford basic necessities.

💡 Our Solution

GigShield AI introduces a parametric insurance system that automatically compensates workers when disruptions occur.

Instead of filing claims manually, our AI engine continuously evaluates risk factors such as weather, traffic, and demand patterns to trigger instant payouts.

⚙️ Core Features

🤖 AI Risk Engine analyzes multiple real-time factors to predict disruptions.
📅 Flexible Plans allow workers to choose affordable weekly coverage.
⚡ Automated Claims ensure instant payouts without paperwork.
🔒 Fraud Detection identifies suspicious patterns using behavioral analysis.
