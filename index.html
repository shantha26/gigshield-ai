Every time it rains in Chennai, Arjun loses half his income — and there is no system that compensates him for it.

Gig workers earn per completed delivery. Disruptions = zero orders = zero pay.
Existing insurance covers health and accidents — not income loss from external events.
Manual claims require paperwork, photos, and days of waiting — impractical for daily workers.
No structured financial safety net exists for India's ~10M gig delivery workforce.

AI / ML Architecture

Three Core ML Models

Machine learning powers risk pricing, threshold decisions, and fraud prevention across the platform.

01
📈

Risk Prediction Model

Analyses historical weather, pollution, and disruption data per city region. Outputs a risk score that determines weekly premium brackets.

rainfall_history temp_extremes aqi_avg flood_freq region_code
02
⚡

Dynamic Premium Engine

Adjusts premiums based on live risk scores. High-risk zones receive higher coverage options at proportionally higher cost, ensuring actuarial fairness.

risk_score city_zone season claim_history
03
🛡️

Fraud Detection Model

Combines rule-based logic with unsupervised clustering to detect GPS spoofing, coordinated fake claims, and unusual behavioral patterns.

gps_trace claim_freq device_id peer_cluster trust_score
